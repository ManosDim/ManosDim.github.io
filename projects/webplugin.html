JP-8000 Supersaw

This is an implementation of the Supersaw oscillator algorithm based on the reverse engineering of the Roland JP-8000 synthesizer. The original analysis comes from the Gearmulator project, which dissected the iconic Roland supersaw sound into its component voices and detuning characteristics.
