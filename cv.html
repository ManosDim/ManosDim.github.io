Biography

Manos Dimogerontakis is a sound artist, audio programmer, and live electronics performer based in Copenhagen. His work explores the intersection of technology and human expression through innovative approaches to sound synthesis, interactive systems, and real-time audio processing.

With a background in both computer science and music technology, Manos develops custom software instruments and interactive installations that challenge conventional boundaries between performer, instrument, and audience. His projects range from algorithmic composition tools to tangible music interfaces that incorporate haptic feedback and gestural control.

As a performer, he specializes in live electronics, creating immersive sonic experiences that blend acoustic and digital elements. His performances often feature real-time audio manipulation, generative processes, and collaborative improvisations with other musicians and technology.

Currently pursuing advanced studies in music technology and sound design, Manos continues to push the boundaries of what's possible when human creativity meets computational power. His work has been featured in various festivals and venues across Europe, contributing to the evolving landscape of contemporary electronic music and sound art.
